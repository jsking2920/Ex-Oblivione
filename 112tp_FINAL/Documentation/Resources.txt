Tcod is a C library originally developed for the roguelike "The Chronicles of Doryen" (TCoD) by a developer known as Jice. 
Python-tcod is the most recent python port of tcod that is actively maintened by Kyle Stewart (HexDecimal).

python-tcod hosted at: https://github.com/tcod/python-tcod/graphs/contributors

From: https://github.com/tcod/tcod
Tcod is a free, fast, portable and uncomplicated API for roguelike developers 
providing an advanced true color console, input, and lots of other utilities frequently used in roguelikes.

installation directions: https://python-tcod.readthedocs.io/en/latest/installation.html
